C. N. R. Rao

Here's a time line of C. N. R. Rao's life:

  • 1934 - Born in Bengalore, India
  • 1951 - He obtained his bachelor's degree from Mysore University, and only at the age of seventeen
  • 1959 - After completion of his graduate studies Rao returned to Bangalore to take up a lecturing position, joining IISC and embarking on an independent research program
  • 1964 - He was elected as a fellow of the Indian Academy of Sciences
  • 1984 to 1994 - Became director of the IISc
  • 1989 - He founded Jawaharlal Nehru Centre for Advanced Scientific Research, Bangalore
  • 2005 - He was appointed Chair of the Scientific Advisory Council to the Indian Prime Minister
  • He shares co-authorship of around 1500 research papers and has co-authored and edited 45 books
